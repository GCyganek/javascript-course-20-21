PROJECT: A ROBOT

In “project” chapters, I’ll stop pummeling you with new theory for a brief mo-ment, and instead we’ll work through a program together. Theory is necessaryto learn to program, but reading and understanding actual programs is just asimportant.

Our project in this chapter is to build an automaton, a little program thatperforms a task in a virtual world. Our automaton will be a mail-delivery robotpicking up and dropping off parcels.
